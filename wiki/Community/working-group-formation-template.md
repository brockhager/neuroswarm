## Working Group Structure & Governance

### Leadership Model
- **Working Group Lead**: Elected by group members, serves 3-month terms
- **Deputy Lead**: Supports lead and handles succession planning
- **Rotating Roles**: Members rotate through different responsibilities

### Decision Making
- **Consensus-Based**: Working groups aim for consensus on decisions
- **Fallback Voting**: Simple majority voting if consensus cannot be reached
- **Escalation Path**: Major decisions escalated to full governance vote

### Accountability Framework
- **Monthly Reports**: Each WG submits progress reports to governance
- **Quarterly Reviews**: Community evaluation of WG performance
- **Success Criteria**: Measurable outcomes tied to WG mission
- **Resource Allocation**: WG budgets approved through governance
