# NST Allocation and Vesting Details

This outlines the distribution of the 21,000,000 NST total supply and the mandatory vesting schedules designed to prevent sudden market dilution from early participants.

## 1. Total Supply Allocation (21,000,000 NST)

The total supply is strategically divided into four main pools, with the majority dedicated to decentralized network security.

| Allocation Segment | Proposed Quantity (NST) | Percentage (%) | Purpose |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Validator Rewards Issuance** | 14,700,000 | 70% | Distributed over time via the 4-year Halving Schedule to incentivize validators and secure the network through Proof-of-Stake. |
| **Ecosystem & Treasury** | 2,100,000 | 10% | Reserved for strategic grants, partnerships, and DAO funding. |
| **Founding Team & Advisors** | 2,100,000 | 10% | Incentivizes long-term core commitment. |
| **Private/Strategic Sales** | 2,100,000 | 10% | Initial funding and liquidity generation. |
| **Total** | **21,000,000** | **100%** | |

## 2. Vesting Schedules (Market Protection)

Vesting protects the market from immediate sell pressure by ensuring tokens allocated to the team and early investors are released gradually over time, enforced by smart contracts.

### A. Founding Team & Advisors (10%)

* **Cliff Period:** **1-year cliff** (No tokens released for the first 12 months).
* **Vesting:** **3-year linear monthly vesting** following the cliff. (Tokens released equally over the next 36 months).

### B. Private/Strategic Sales (10%)

* **Cliff Period:** **6-month cliff** (No tokens released for the first 6 months).
* **Vesting:** **2-year linear monthly vesting** following the cliff. (Tokens released equally over the next 24 months).
