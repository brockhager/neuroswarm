## How to Register

**Step 1: Fill out the form**
```
https://neuroswarm.io/validator-signup
```

**Step 2: Join Discord**
```
https://discord.gg/neuroswarm
```

**Step 3: Prepare hardware**
- Minimum: 4 cores, 8GB RAM, 50GB SSD
- Recommended: 8 cores, 16GB RAM, 100GB SSD, RTX 3060

**Step 4: Stake NST**
- Minimum: 5,000 NST stake required
- Foundation provides bridge from Ethereum (if needed)
